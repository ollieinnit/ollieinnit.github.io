Why this club?

This club provides an excellent and safe opportunity for young people in our community, irrespective of their age, ability, sex, religion or ethnic background, to develop their personal skills, confidence and self-esteem through a shared love of football for everyone being inclusive and respectful of those within the teams no matter the age

Club history

Galmington dragons was established in 1998 from a simple kick about in vivary park to a club with around 350 members players can join from U5s to U16s then progress to the mens team. currently the football club is being chaired by Martin lever since 2008 with Pete kirby as Treasurer,Paul Bradshaw as a secetary and Alan hilton as a welfare officer
